Driven by values, delivering on a vision, determined to innovate

We at Jai Mata Di Enterprises are doing the work of IRON SLAG PROCESSING, when we first started the work the results ware shocking as we recovered almost 8% metal of the slag weight, thereafter the plan came to our mind to do this work commercially and so the work started. In this we process the waste (slag) generated during the melting of iron and steel in any kind of melting furnaces (EAF, EOF, BOF, MBF, INDUCTION, CONVERTOR, Etc.). When we first started the work way back in 2002, from then we have came a long way by converting the manual system to atomization and mechanizing the entire process. By doing so we have not only reduced the working cost but has also increased the recovery of metal from slag.
